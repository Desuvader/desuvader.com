About me

My name is Dennis “Desuvader” Jin. I am a designer and developer from Sweden. I believe, in order to create coherent products, one has to refine one's skills in both design and programming.

I am currently studying computer science at the Faculty of Engineering (LTH) in Lund, Sweden. Feel free to download my résumé or read about my everyday life on Twitter.
